A college fest is also called a cultural or technical fest and it is big annual event organized by students in a college . It is one of the most exciting parts of campus life where students show their talents, enjoy activities, and interact with other colleges. Provide a platform to showcase student talent Encourage creativity and teamwork Give relaxation from regular academic routine Improve confidence and communication skills Promote interaction between different colleges Fests help in overall personality development and social interaction among students it is a complete learning and celebration experience for students. It brings together creativity, leadership, teamwork, and fun on one platform. Most colleges treat the fest as the biggest event of the academic year.
